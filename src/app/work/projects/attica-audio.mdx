## Overview

Design and delivery of a new website for Attica Audio - a recording studio in beautiful Donegal, Ireland.

## Key Features

- **Easy content management via Sanity CMS**: The site is developed alongside a customized Sanity CMS, giving the client a custom content management experience tailored to their requirements.

## Technologies Used

- **Svelte**: Clean and simple component-based frontend
- **Sanity CMS**: Customized CMS for easy content management
